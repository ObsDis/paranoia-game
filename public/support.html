Paranoia Support

Need help, want to report a problem, or want to flag offensive content? We respond within 24 hours.

Email: support@playparanoia.org

Common requests

  • Delete my account: Open the app, tap your name on the home screen, then tap "Delete account". You can also email us.
  • Report a question or player: Email us with the details. Include the room code if you have it.
  • Bug reports: Tell us what device you're on and what happened. Screenshots help.
  • Forgot password: Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in screen.

Content rules

The following are not allowed in custom questions or display names: hate speech, slurs, sexual content involving minors, threats, harassment of real people, or anything that targets a person based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, or disability. Violations will be removed and accounts may be suspended.
