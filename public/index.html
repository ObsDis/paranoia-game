The party game where you never know what was asked... unless the coin decides.

How to Play

  1. A question is whispered to one player
  2. They answer by pointing at someone in the circle
  3. A coin is flipped: Heads = question revealed, Tails = stays secret forever
  4. The pointed-at person may never know why...
